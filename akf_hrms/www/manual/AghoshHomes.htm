Aghosh Homes User Manual

Overview

Welcome to the Aghosh Homes module of XpertERP, a customized solution designed to manage the unique needs of Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan's Aghosh Homes. This module integrates hostel and school management functionalities to support the well-being and education of Aghosh residents. It centralizes resident information, manages accommodation, tracks attendance, facilitates academic activities, and ensures effective communication. This guide will provide an overview of the module's features, with the understanding that it will be tailored to AKFP's specific requirements.

Accessing the Aghosh Homes Module

To access the Aghosh Homes module in XpertERP:

  1. Log In: Open your web browser and go to the XpertERP login page (e.g., https://your-akfp-xperterp-url.com).
  2. Enter Credentials: Input your assigned Username and Password.
  3. Dashboard: After logging in, you'll see your XpertERP Dashboard.
  4. Navigate to Aghosh Homes:
    • Click on the "Modules" icon (usually a grid or squares) in the top-left corner.
    • Select "Aghosh Homes" from the list of modules.
    • Alternatively, use the Search Bar at the top by typing "Aghosh Homes" and selecting "Aghosh Homes."

Key Features & Common Tasks

The XpertERP Aghosh Homes module is designed to support the following key areas:

Resident Management

  • Resident Profiles: Maintaining comprehensive records of each resident, including personal information, contact details, medical history, and emergency contacts.
  • Admission/Registration: Managing the intake and registration process for new residents.
  • Guardianship Information: Recording details of guardians or sponsors for each resident.

7.3.2 Accommodation Management

  • Room/Bed Allocation: Assigning residents to specific rooms or beds, considering factors such as age, gender, and special needs.
  • Room Inventory: Tracking the furniture and other assets assigned to each room.
  • Maintenance Requests: Managing and tracking maintenance requests for rooms and facilities.

Attendance Management

  • Attendance Tracking: Recording daily attendance of residents, both for hostel and school activities.
  • Leave Management: Managing resident leave requests and approvals.
  • Visitor Management: Tracking visitor access to the Aghosh Homes.

School Management (If Applicable)

  • Academic Records: Maintaining records of resident's academic performance, grades, and progress.
  • Curriculum Management: Managing lesson plans, assignments, and learning resources.
  • Fee Management: (If applicable) Managing school fees and payments.
  • Reporting: Generating report cards and transcripts.

Communication

  • Notifications: Sending announcements and updates to residents, guardians, and staff.
  • Messaging: Facilitating communication between residents, staff, and guardians.

Reporting

  • Resident Reports: Generating reports on resident demographics, attendance, and other relevant data.
  • Financial Reports: (If applicable) Generating reports on fee collection and expenses.

Setups & Configurations

  • Resident Categories: Defining different categories of residents.
  • Room Types: Defining different types of rooms available.
  • Fee Structures: (If applicable) Configuring fee structures for school or hostel services.
  • User Roles & Permissions: Defining user roles and access levels within the module.
