Human Resources

Overview

1.1 Introduction to the HR Module

Welcome to the Human Resources (HR) module of XpertERP, your customized solution for Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan. This module is designed to streamline all essential HR processes, from managing employee records and leave applications to payroll processing and performance evaluations. By centralizing HR data and automating routine tasks, XpertERP empowers AKFP staff and managers to focus on our core mission while ensuring efficient, transparent, and accurate HR operations. This guide will walk you through the most commonly used features, complemented by short training videos for a visual demonstration.

Accessing the HR Module

To access the HR module in XpertERP:

  1. Log in: Open your web browser and navigate to the XpertERP login page (e.g., https://your-akfp-xperterp-url.com).
  2. Enter Credentials: Input your assigned Username and Password.
  3. Dashboard: After successful login, you will land on your XpertERP Dashboard.
  4. Navigate to HR:
    • Click on the "Modules" icon (usually a grid or squares) in the top-left corner.
    • Select "Human Resources" from the list of modules.
    • Alternatively, use the Search Bar at the top by typing "HR" and selecting "Human Resources."

Key Features & Common Tasks

The XpertERP HR module supports various functionalities. Here are some of the key areas you'll interact with:

Employee Management

This section allows HR personnel to manage comprehensive employee data and for employees to view their own profile.

  • Viewing Employee Records:
    • For HR: Navigate to HR > Employee. You will see a list of all employees. Click on an employee's name to view their detailed profile (e.g., personal details, contact information, employment history, assigned department/branch).
    • For Employees: Log in and navigate to "My Profile" or HR > Employee (you will only see your own record).
  • Updating Personal Details:
    • For Employees: Some fields (e.g., contact number, personal email) can be updated by the employee directly in their profile. Click "Edit" at the top right of your profile, make changes, and click "Save." For sensitive changes (e.g., bank account, CNIC), contact the HR department.

Video: Short video demonstrating how HR navigates to employee list and opens a profile. Then, show an employee editing their contact details on their own profile.

Leave Management

Manage your leave applications and approvals efficiently through the system.

  • Applying for Leave:
    1. Navigate to HR > Leave Application.
    2. Click "+ Add Leave Application".
    3. Select "Leave Type" (e.g., Annual, Sick, Casual).
    4. Enter "From Date" and "To Date". The system will calculate the number of days.
    5. (Optional) Add a "Reason for Leave."
    6. Click "Save".
    7. Click "Submit" to send it for approval. Your supervisor will be notified.
  • Viewing Leave Balance:
    • Navigate to HR > Leave Application. Your available leave balance for each type will typically be displayed on your dashboard or when you create a new leave application.
  • Approving/Rejecting Leave (For Managers):
    • You will receive a notification when an employee submits a leave application.
    • Navigate to HR > Leave Application.
    • Open the pending leave application, review the details.
    • Click "Approve" or "Reject" and add a comment if necessary.

Video: Short video showing an employee applying for leave step-by-step. Then, a quick clip of a manager approving that leave application.

Payroll Management (For HR/Finance Personnel)

This section outlines the basic process for managing payroll. Note: Payroll is generally handled by authorized HR/Finance personnel only.

  • Creating Salary Slips:
    1. Navigate to HR > Salary Slip.
    2. Click "+ Add Salary Slip."
    3. Select the "Employee" and the "Payroll Period."
    4. The system will pre-populate earnings and deductions based on configured salary structures. Review and make any necessary adjustments.
    5. Click "Save" then "Submit".
  • Generating Payroll Reports:
    • Navigate to HR > Reports and select relevant payroll reports (e.g., Salary Register, Provident Fund Statement) for review and submission.

Video: Short video showing HR creating one salary slip and briefly navigating to payroll reports. Emphasize that this is for authorized personnel.

Performance Management

  • Submitting Performance Reviews (For Employees):
    • During designated review periods, you may receive a notification or find a "Performance Appraisal" document under HR > Performance Appraisal.
    • Open the document, fill in your self-assessment based on the defined KPIs/objectives.
    • Click "Save" then "Submit" when complete.

Video: Short video showing an employee opening a performance appraisal and filling out a few fields.

Reports

The HR module provides various reports to help analyze data and support decision-making.

  • Accessing Reports:
    • Navigate to HR > Reports.
    • Select the desired report from the list (e.g., Employee Headcount, Leave Balance Summary, Employee Attendance).
    • Apply filters (e.g., by Department, Date Range) as needed and click "Refresh" to generate the report.
