FAQs

 

1.       Q: What is the AKFP ERP system?

A: The AKFP ERP system is an integrated software solution designed to streamline and manage Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan's core operations, specifically covering Human Resources, Stock Management, Project Management and Accounting functions from a centralized platform.

2.       Q: How do I log in to the ERP system?

A: You can log in to the ERP system using your assigned username and password on the designated login portal. If you forget your password, please use the "Forgot Password" link or contact the IT Support team.

3.       Q: Who do I contact for technical issues or system errors?

A: For any technical issues, system errors, or access problems, please contact the AKFP IT Support or log the ticket to Jira.

4.       Q: Is there a mobile application for the ERP system?

A: No, currently the ERP system is accessible via web browser on desktop devices. Also, it can be accessed through mobile browser on smart phones.

5.       Q: How do I ensure data security and confidentiality when using the ERP?

A: Always log out when you finish your session, never share your login credentials, and ensure you are working on a secure network. Adhere to AKFP's data privacy and security policies outlined in your employee handbook.

6.       Q: How can I view my personal employee information?

A: You can view and update certain aspects of your personal employee information, such as contact details, by navigating to the "My Profile" section within the HR module. For changes to sensitive data (e.g., bank details, CNIC), please contact the HR department directly with supporting documentation.

7.       Q: How do I apply for leave using the HR module?

A: To apply for leave, go to the "Leave Management" section, select "Apply for Leave," choose your leave type, dates, and provide any necessary remarks. Your application will then be routed to your designated supervisor for approval.

8.       Q: Can I view my past payslips in the system?

A: Yes, you can access and download your historical payslips from the "Payroll" or "My Documents" section within the HR module.

9.       Q: How do I submit my performance review or self-assessment?

A: During the performance review cycle, you will receive a notification or access to the "Performance Management" section where you can complete and submit your self-assessment form as per the defined timeline.

10.   Q: What should I do if my supervisor is not listed for leave approval?

A: If your designated supervisor is not correctly listed for leave approvals, please contact the HR department immediately to update your reporting structure in the system.

11.   Q: How do I record newly received goods or donations into the system?

A: Navigate to the "Goods Receipt Note (GRN)" section, enter the relevant details such as supplier/donor, item specifics, quantity, and date. Ensure all supporting documentation is accurate before saving.

12.   Q: What is an SKU and why is it important?

A: An SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) is a unique alphanumeric code assigned to each distinct item in our inventory. It is crucial for accurate tracking, identification, and management of stock levels within the system.

13.   Q: How do I issue stock for a project or program?

A: Go to the "Goods Issue Note (GIN)" section, select the items and quantities required, specify the destination project/department, and ensure proper authorization before completing the transaction.

14.   Q: Can I check the current stock level of a specific item?

A: Yes, you can view real-time stock levels for any item by using the "Stock Inquiry" or "Inventory Report" function within the Stock Management module.

15.   Q: What should I do if there's a discrepancy between physical stock and system records?

A: Immediately report any stock discrepancies to your supervisor and the designated inventory controller. A stock reconciliation process will need to be initiated to investigate and correct the variance.

16.   Q: How do I enter a new vendor invoice for payment?

A: To enter a new vendor invoice, navigate to the "Accounts Payable (AP)" section, select "Invoice Entry," and input all required details including vendor name, invoice number, date, amount, and corresponding general ledger accounts.

17.   Q: Where can I generate a financial report like the Income Statement or Balance Sheet?

A: All standard financial reports, including the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement, can be generated from the "Financial Reporting" section within the Accounting module. Select your desired report and parameters.

18.   Q: How do I record a donation received in cash or via bank transfer?

A: For cash donations, use the "Cash Receipt" function. For bank transfers, use the "Bank Receipt" or "Journal Entry" function to record the funds, ensuring proper categorization and linking to the relevant fund or project.

19.   Q: What is a Fund Centre/Cost Centre and why is it important in the system?

A: A Fund Centre (or Cost Centre) is a dimension used to track expenses and revenues related to specific programs, projects, or departments within AKFP. It is crucial for accurate fund accounting, budgeting, and reporting on the utilization of resources for specific initiatives.

20.   Q: Who should I contact if I have a question about a specific general ledger account or a financial transaction?

A: For queries regarding general ledger accounts, specific financial transactions, or accounting principles, please contact the AKFP Finance Department or your designated accounting supervisor.
