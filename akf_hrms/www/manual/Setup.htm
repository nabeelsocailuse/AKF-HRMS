XpertERP Module Setups & Configurations

Solution Name: XpertERP Client: Alkhidmat Foundation Pakistan (AKFP)

Overview

This chapter provides an overview of the key setup and configuration areas within XpertERP for the Human Resources, Stock (Inventory & Fixed Assets), Project Management, and Accounts modules. These setups define the core rules, master data, and workflows that govern how the system operates for AKFP. Understanding these configurations is vital for System Administrators and authorized departmental heads to ensure the system aligns with AKFP's operational policies and financial requirements.

Important Note: Modifying system setups requires appropriate permissions and a thorough understanding of their impact. Always exercise caution and, if unsure, consult with the XpertERP technical team or System Administrator.

Human Resources (HR) Module Setups

Key configurations for HR operations.

  • Employee Master Data:
    • Employee Categories: Defining types of employees (e.g., "Permanent," "Contractor," "Volunteer").
    • Designations & Departments: Setting up the organizational hierarchy and roles within AKFP.
    • Grades & Salary Structures: Configuring different pay scales and components (e.g., Basic Salary, Allowances, Deductions) that form the basis for payroll.
  • Leave Management:
    • Leave Types: Defining various leave types (e.g., "Annual Leave," "Sick Leave," "Maternity Leave") and their rules (e.g., entitlement, carry-forward, maximum limits).
    • Holiday List: Setting up official holidays for accurate leave calculations.
  • Payroll & Attendance:
    • Attendance Settings: Configuring shifts, attendance tracking methods, and overtime rules.
    • Payroll Periods: Defining monthly or bi-monthly payroll cycles.
    • Deductions & Benefits: Setting up Provident Fund, EOBI, or other organizational-specific deductions/benefits.
  • Performance Management:
    • Appraisal Cycles & Templates: Defining periods for performance reviews and the structure of appraisal forms.

Stock Management Module Setups

Configurations related to Inventory and Fixed Assets.

Inventory Setups

  • Warehouses:
    • Defining Warehouse Structure: Setting up physical storage locations (e.g., "Main Warehouse - Islamabad," "Lahore Project Store," "Quetta Regional Hub") and their hierarchy.
  • Item Master Data:
    • Item Groups: Categorizing items (e.g., "Food Items," "Medical Supplies," "Shelter Kits").
    • Units of Measure (UOM): Defining how items are counted (e.g., "Pcs," "Kg," "Liters," "Cartons").
    • Item Attributes: Adding specific properties to items (e.g., "Brand," "Color," "Size").
  • Stock Settings:
    • Naming Series: Customizing prefixes for Stock Entry documents (e.g., "STK-REC-", "STK-ISS-").
    • Default Accounts: Linking inventory transactions to specific accounts in the Chart of Accounts.

Fixed Asset Setups

  • Asset Categories:
    • Defining Asset Types: Setting up categories for assets (e.g., "Vehicles," "IT Equipment," "Office Furniture").
    • Depreciation Methods: Configuring how depreciation is calculated for each asset category (e.g., "Straight Line," "Declining Balance").
  • Asset Naming Series: Customizing prefixes for Asset IDs (e.g., "AKFP-IT-", "AKFP-VEH-").

Project Management Module Setups

Configurations for project planning and execution.

  • Project Types:
    • Defining Project Categories: Setting up different types of projects (e.g., "Disaster Response," "Health Program," "Orphan Care").
  • Project Templates:
    • Standard Project Structures: Creating predefined templates with common tasks and milestones for repetitive project types to standardize planning.
  • Task Defaults:
    • Default Task Statuses: Defining the lifecycle of a task (e.g., "Open," "Working," "Completed," "On Hold").
  • Timesheet Settings: (If timesheets are used for project cost tracking)
    • Configuring work week, holidays, and hourly rates for staff.

Accounts Management Module Setups

Crucial financial configurations for AKFP.

  • Chart of Accounts (CoA):
    • Designing the CoA: Structuring the accounts for Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Income, and Expenses to meet AKFP's financial reporting and donor requirements. This includes specific accounts for various donation types, project revenues/expenses, and administrative costs.
    • Cost Centers & Projects as Dimensions: Ensuring that every relevant expense and income account is linked to "Cost Centre" and/or "Project" dimensions for robust programmatic financial tracking and reporting.
  • Company & Fiscal Year:
    • Setting up AKFP as a Company: Basic company information and legal entity details.
    • Fiscal Year End: Defining the financial year for reporting.
  • Tax & Withholding Categories: (If applicable)
    • Configuring local tax regulations for purchases, sales, or payroll.
  • Bank Accounts & Payment Gateways:
    • Configuring AKFP Bank Accounts: Linking actual bank accounts to the system for payment and receipt processing.
    • Payment Gateways: (If integrated for online donations) Setting up online payment processing.
  • Journal Entry Rules:
    • Naming Series for Journals: Customizing prefixes for different types of journal entries.
    • Default Accounts: Setting up default accounts for various transaction types.

User Permissions & Roles

While not module-specific, proper user permissions are fundamental to all setups.

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):
    • Defining Roles: Setting up roles like "HR Manager," "Warehouse Officer," "Project Manager," "Accountant," "System Administrator."
    • Assigning Permissions: Granting specific "read," "write," "create," "submit," "cancel" permissions for documents and modules based on each role's responsibilities.
  • User Accounts:
    • Creating User Accounts: Setting up individual user logins and assigning appropriate roles.
